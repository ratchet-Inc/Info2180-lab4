Personalized movie recommendations

Our aim

We all know that finding a good movie that you are most definitely sure you'll enjoy can be a troublesome task.

Our Aim

The aim of the system is to provide movie recommendations that it believes will be of interest to each individual user, therefore users can find titles that are worthy of being watched with minimal effort.

Similarly to other major content streaming platforms, recommendations that are provided are optimized to match each user’s profile. We aim to mimic this behavior with our system. Though it is expected that the recommendations produced by our system will not yield accurate results as the major dominating platforms of the industry, it is a start.
